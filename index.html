Biography of
Minister Mercy Chinwo

Mercy Nnenda Chinwo was born on the 5th of September 1990. She is the first child and the first daughter of six children. Mercy Chinwo is a Nigerian gospel musician, actress, and songwriter. She hails from Port Harcourt, Rivers State. Mercy Chinwo started her musical career at a very early age. She started music in her church’s children’s choir and later migrated to the adult choir. Mercy Chinwo was brought up by her mother after she lost her dad at a very young age.
